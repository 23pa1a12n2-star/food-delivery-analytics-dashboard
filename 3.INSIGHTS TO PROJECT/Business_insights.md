
# 📈 Phase 3: Visualization Insights

Here correspond the 9 visualizations generated to our 3 core stakeholders (Rahul, Priya, Neha). 

### 1. KPI Dashboard (Summary)
**Business Insight (All Stakeholders):** The platform processed ~₹35 million in aggregate revenue across 50,000 orders, demonstrating a robust, high-volume baseload of consistent ordering demand.

### 2. Flavors Across Cities
**Business Insight (Rahul - Analyst):** Cuisine preference is remarkably uniform across all 5 metro cities, revealing a broad, untailored palate where no single city dictates a niche culinary market.

### 3. Order Distribution (Age Group & Restaurant Segment)
**Business Insight (Rahul - Analyst):** Customers across all age bands distribute their orders evenly among casual, fast-food, and premium segments, indicating highly diversified eating behaviors free of demographic skew.

### 4. Cuisine Preferences
**Business Insight (Rahul - Analyst):** With every major cuisine commanding a near-perfect ~16-17% market share, promotional marketing can safely push any food category without fear of disrupting a dominant customer favorite.

### 5. Operational Analysis (Company Type vs. Meal Type)
**Business Insight (Priya - Ops):** Both Corporate and Individual patrons overwhelmingly drive order velocity during Lunch and Dinner, solidifying these two periods as critical dual peaks for logistics and driver allocation.

### 6. Customer Satisfaction (Rating by Meal Type)
**Business Insight (Neha - Customer):** Customer satisfaction remains flat and underwhelming (averaging 2.6/5 stars) across all meal types, pointing to chronic platform-wide operational delays rather than brief, isolated rush-hour breakdowns.

### 7. City Demand
**Business Insight (Priya - Ops):** Geographic order demand is flawlessly balanced (~10,000 orders per metro), making fleet scaling and supply chain logistics a replicable strategy city-by-city rather than dealing with asymmetrical volume spikes.

### 8. Revenue Contribution (Order Value by Meal Type)
**Business Insight (Rahul - Analyst):** Lunch and Dinner predictably account for roughly 70% of total revenue, confirming these primary meal times as the core engines of profitability versus the lower-value Breakfast and Snacking periods.

### 9. Delivery Performance (Time Taken)
**Business Insight (Priya - Ops & Neha - Customer):** Delivery times exhibit a heavy cluster between 40 to 60 minutes, which starkly violates our implicit 30-minute benchmark and directly drags down the overarching customer rating metric.
